FRAUD ALERT

Beware of individuals posing as XPO human resources personnel when you apply for positions online.

 

Fraudulent emails and fake XPO career postings and job advertisements have been reported. If you respond to these types of hoaxes, you may be asked to provide personal information, such as your social security number, driver’s license number, tax information and banking information, along with seemingly legitimate information about your goals and skills. If you encounter one of these fraudulent communications, do not provide personal information. Please go to www.XPO.ethicspoint.com to report it and consider alerting law enforcement as well. Thank you.
